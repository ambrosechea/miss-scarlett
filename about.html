the beginning

Miss Scarlett was founded by Melissa So in late 2019 with a vision to create bridal gowns that feel modern, beautifully crafted, and accessible to brides everywhere.

THE BRAND TODAY

What began as a passion for contemporary bridal design soon found its place in boutiques around the world.
